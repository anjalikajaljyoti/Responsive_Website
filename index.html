Coffees

Espresso

Espresso is the foundation of many coffee varieties.Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee produced by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely ground coffee beans.

Americano

An Americano is made by diluting an espresso shot with hot water.Caffè americano, also known as americano or American, is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso shot with hot water at a 1:3 to 1:4 ratio.

Latte

Caffè latte, also known as caffè e latte, caffellatte, or caffelatte, commonly shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk, traditionally served in a glass.

cappuccino

A cappuccino is similar to a latte, but this espresso drink involves less steamed milk and more foam.Cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk including a layer of milk foam.
